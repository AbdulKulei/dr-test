About Me

I'm Dr. Jamal Abade

General Surgeon/laparascopic Surgeon

I am a highly skilled and enthusiastic consultant general and laparoscopic surgeon with over four years of experience. My professional journey has been marked by a commitment to providing high-quality patient care and advancing surgical practices. Having completed my residency training in General Surgery at Mbarara University in Uganda, I have honed my skills in a diverse range of surgical procedures. My subsequent fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery at the World Laparoscopy Hospital further solidified my expertise in laparoscopic techniques. Currently, I am serving as a Consultant General and Laparoscopic Surgeon at Wajir County Referral Hospital. In this role, I have successfully performed numerous complex surgical procedures, including laparoscopic cholecystectomy, appendectomy, and hernia repair. I am passionate about utilizing minimally invasive techniques to minimize patient discomfort and expedite recovery times. With a strong foundation in general surgery and a specialization in minimally invasive techniques, I am confident in my ability to contribute significantly to your esteemed institution
